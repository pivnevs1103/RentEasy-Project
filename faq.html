Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Account & Billing

To update your billing information or payment method, follow these steps:
  • Log in to your account with your registered email and password.
  • Navigate to the Account Settings or Billing section in your user dashboard.
  • Under the Payment Methods section, you’ll see an option to Edit or Update Payment Method. Click on it.
  • Enter your new credit or debit card details, or select a different payment method (such as PayPal if available).
  • Confirm the changes by clicking the Save Changes button.
After updating, your new payment method will be used for all future billing cycles. If you encounter any issues during this process, please contact our Support Team for assistance.

If you're being charged but cannot access your account, here’s what you can do:
  • Reset Your Password: Go to the login page and click on the “Forgot Password?” link. Enter your registered email to receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions to set a new password.
  • Check Your Email: Ensure you're using the correct email associated with your account. Sometimes, multiple email addresses can cause confusion.
  • Check Subscription Status: If your payment method is valid, your subscription should remain active. Log in and verify the Subscription status in the Billing section.
  • If none of these steps work, contact our Billing Support Team with proof of payment (e.g., transaction ID or receipt), and we will help you regain access or resolve any billing issues promptly.

To cancel your subscription, follow these steps:
  • Log in to your account and go to the Account Settings or Subscription section.
  • Click on “Manage Subscription” and select the option to Cancel Subscription.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the cancellation. You will receive an email confirmation once your subscription is canceled.
Refund Policy: If you cancel within the first 14 days of your billing cycle and haven’t used any premium services, you may be eligible for a full refund. After 14 days, cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle, and you will not receive a refund for the current period. You will retain access to premium features until the end of the cycle. For further clarification or to request a refund, please contact our Support Team with your account details.

Website Issues

Why is the website not loading or taking too long to respond?

Website loading issues can occur due to a variety of reasons. Here are some steps you can take to resolve this:

  1. Check Your Internet Connection: Ensure your device is connected to a stable internet connection. Try visiting other websites to verify if the issue is specific to our site.
  2. Clear Browser Cache: Sometimes cached files or cookies can cause issues. Clear your browser's cache and cookies:
    • Windows/Linux: Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete
    • Mac: Press Cmd + Shift + Delete
  3. Try a Different Browser: If the issue persists, try accessing the website on a different browser (e.g., Chrome, Firefox, Safari) or device.
  4. Disable Extensions or VPNs: Browser extensions, VPNs, or ad blockers might interfere with website loading. Temporarily disable them and refresh the page.
  5. Check Server Status: Occasionally, the website may be down for maintenance or experiencing server issues. Check our official Twitter account or Status Page for real-time updates.

If none of these steps work, please contact our Support Team and provide details about the issue, including any error messages.
